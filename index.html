How to Play

Sort every bubble in three easy steps

The rules are simple, but each new level gives you a fresh puzzle to solve.

1

Choose a tube

Tap a tube to select the bubble sitting at the very top.

2

Move the bubble

Place it into an empty tube or on top of another bubble with the same color.

3

Complete the level

Keep sorting until every tube contains only one matching bubble color.
